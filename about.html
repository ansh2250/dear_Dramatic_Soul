About Us

Dear Dramatic Soul is an Indian premium brand weaving stories of elegance through its clothing and accessories, crafted with both artistry and integrity. At its essence, DDS is a journey towards the purest form of love, the kind that resides at the heart of our being. Our mission is to touch souls, creating a sanctuary where authenticity blooms without fear, and where healing and love flow freely among all.

Each creation is a tribute to the love, grace, and confidence that dwell within our customers. We cherish the journey over the destination, nurturing a realm where love is a constant companion, life is a vibrant embrace, and each moment is savored with depth and joy.

Our Philosophy & Commitment

Welcome to Dear Dramatic Soul, where handcrafted fashion meets personal expression. Our brand is dedicated to celebrating your unique essence through meticulously crafted clothing and accessories that resonate with your inner self.

We believe that elegance should not come at the cost of ethical values. Our commitment to ethical business practices means that our production processes are environmentally friendly and socially responsible. When you choose Dear Dramatic Soul, you support a brand that values both style and sustainability.

Explore Timeless Fashion

At Dear Dramatic Soul, we believe that true style never goes out of fashion. Our collections feature timeless designs that blend classic elegance with contemporary flair.

Owning an artisanal piece represents a mark of luxury, combining both stunning beauty and a commitment to responsibility. Our sustainable sourcing, fair labor practices, and eco-friendly production processes reflect our dedication to making fashion that respects both people and the planet.

The Soul Behind the Brand

RAJBALA BHAGAT

Co-founder | Creative Head

Vibing to the Frequency of Love

My life's journey has always fascinated me, and I've been curious to know where it's leading me. Upon reaching the core of my existence, I realized that the love I always hoped to receive from others already existed within me. Vibrating on this frequency of love is a choice I've made to practice every day because it allows me the blessing of feeling the universal presence of love, joy, and peace. My purpose here is to share the gift of self-love with the world while embracing the rhythm of life and promising to love myself unconditionally.

PRITI KUMARI

Co-founder | Operations

Living life on own terms

I never felt content with my career path, even after earning my master's degree. Listening to others caused me to lose my own identity. I did what I had to do as a friend, sister, and daughter, but I never paid attention to my inner voice. I had to embark on a journey to discover my true calling by going beyond the conventional expectations of society. Although the future is uncertain, I am determined to embrace my independence and live my life according to my own values and rules, which set me apart.
